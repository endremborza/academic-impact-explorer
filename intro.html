In a world where every country wants to become a knowledge powerhouse, we need more than university rankings.

Universities are a source of cultural and economic growth. They attract talent, educate the population, and help produce important innovations

But the impact of universities cannot be reduced to a single number. Knowledge is highly specific, and so is the impact of universities.

Universities specialize in fields and have important local networks of collaboration. Isn't it time we understand them in their right context.

Consider three similarly “ranked” universities. The Swedish University of Agricultural Sciences (SLU), the University of Belgrade, in Serbia, and the University of Lille, in France.
